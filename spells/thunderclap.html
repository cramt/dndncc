Thunderclap

Level: 0

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 5 Feet

Components: S

Duration: Instantaneous


You create a burst of thunderous sound that can be heard up to 100 feet away. Each creature within range, other than you, must make a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 thunder damage.The spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
