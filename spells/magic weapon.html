Magic Weapon

Level: 2

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: Bonus

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, Up To 1 Hour

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

At Higher Levels. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.
