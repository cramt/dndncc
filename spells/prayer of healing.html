Prayer Of Healing

Level: 2

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 10 Min.

Range: 30 Feet

Components: V

Duration: Instantaneous


Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range each regain hit points equal to 2d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.


At Higher Levels. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
