Reincarnate

Level: 5

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 Hr.

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (rare oils and unguents worth at least 1,000 gp, which the spell consumes)

Duration: Instantaneous


You touch a dead humanoid or a piece of a dead humanoid. Provided that the creature has been dead no longer than 10 days, the spell forms a new adult body for it and then calls the soul to enter that body. If the target's soul isn't free or willing to do so, the spell fails.The magic fashions a new body for the creature to inhabit, which likely causes the creature's race to change.
