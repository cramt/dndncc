Frozen Blade (Homebrew)

Level: 0

School: Evocation

At Higher Levels. At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d8 cold damage to the target on a hit. The damage roll increases by 1d8 at 11th level (2d8) and 17th level (3d8).
